---

### Voice Rules

- {{VOICE_TONE}}
- No dashes (em dash, en dash, mid-sentence hyphens) — use commas, periods, or rephrase
- No buzzwords: "game-changer", "paradigm shift", "in today's fast-paced landscape" — never
- No motivational filler: "believe in yourself", "hustle harder" — never
- Confident but not arrogant. Direct, not corporate. Human, not polished to death
- Every performance claim needs a number, or explicitly flag "directional / not measured yet"

---

### Post Structure

1. **Hook** — Data point, insight, or sharp observation. Cite the source.
2. **Expand** — Take a clear POV. What does this actually mean?
3. **3 concrete steps** or a direct recommendation
4. **CTA** — Question or invitation. Short. Not generic ("What do you think?").

### Formatting Rules

- Line breaks between every paragraph — no walls of text
- Short sentences. 1-2 per line max.
- 3-5 bullet points max if using bullets
- No hashtag dumps — 2-3 targeted hashtags at the end only
- Character target: 1,000-1,500 characters (sweet spot for LinkedIn algorithm)

---

### Save After Writing

Every post must be saved immediately after writing:
- File: `Content/linkedin/linkedin_post_{slug}.md`
- Number sequentially if tracking post history

---

### Rules

- Never finish writing a post without saving it
- Every post must connect to one of the content pillars above
- Do not create an image unless explicitly asked — flag that an image is needed
