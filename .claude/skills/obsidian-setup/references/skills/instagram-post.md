### Voice Rules

- {{VOICE_TONE}}
- Natural, human, warm — same voice as LinkedIn but more casual
- Short sentences. Real thoughts, not performances.
- No corporate tone, no buzzwords

---

### Structure (educational / brand content)

1. **Hook** — first line stops the scroll. Bold claim, specific number, or surprising insight.
2. **Body** — short paragraphs, line breaks. Max 5-7 lines visible before "more".
3. **CTA** — question or invitation. "What would you build with this?" not "Follow for more."
4. **Hashtags** — 5-10 targeted, at the end. Mix of niche and broad.

### Structure (personal / lifestyle content)

1. Authentic moment or observation — caption should feel like a real thought
2. Short. One idea. No need to sell anything.
3. Hashtags optional — 3-5 max if used
